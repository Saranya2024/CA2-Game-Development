Game Instructions

Here are the rules of the game:

  • You're given a set of attempts to guess the word correctly before the game starts.
  • You can make guesses by entering letters, depending on the complexity of the word.
  • Successfully guessing the word within the allotted attempts results in winning the game and earns 3 points.
  • Failing to guess the word within the attempts leads to losing the game.
  • ALL THE BEST
